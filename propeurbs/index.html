WHAT IS PROPEURBS?

PROPEURBS is a two-hour business workshop for creative entrepreneurs, small business owners and anyone looking to Spark Results. We will teach you how to define your Brand, master Social Media tools and fund your business.


The workshop is designed for the dreamers and the do-ers out there. Whether you have already started a business or blog, or you have tiny seed you’re looking to plant and develop in the years to come, this workshop is for you. Or, perhaps you’ve had a business for many years but might like to revamp and freshen up the brand.


The workshop

Here are some elements of the day:
  • • Certificate of participation and completion.
  • • Collaborative and supportive work environment. Many of you will start as strangers and leave as close friends.
  • • Basic knowledge of graphic. This will help you to manage most of your design work yourself.
