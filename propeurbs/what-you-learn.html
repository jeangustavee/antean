WHAT YOU WILL LEARN?

PROPEURBS is a one-day workshop for creative entrepreneurs, small business owners and anyone looking to Spark Results. We will teach you how to create a Business Plan, define your Brand, master Social Media tools and provide the right tools to raise capital for your business.


BUILDING YOUR PERSONAL or BUSINESS BRAND
Learn the steps to create a great brand
Brainstorm popular brands and establish why they are successful
Discover what you’d like people to be saying about your brand
Learn the basics about fonts and logos
Come up with a colour palette for your brand
Create your ‘voice’
Define your Target Audience and establish your niche market
Incorporate every aspect of your business into building your brand

SOCIAL MEDIA SKILLS + TOOLS
Learn which tools your Target Audience is using
Create your own Facebook brand page
Boost your Instagram page
The difference between Facebook Profiles and Facebook Pages
Find out how to use Facebook Insights for your Page
Setup your Twitter and learn Best Practices
Master hashtags and when to use them
Create a social media strategy and campaign
Learn how to reach a greater audience

TAKE BETTER PHOTOGRAPHS FOR SOCIAL MEDIA
Basic photography techniques
Experimenting with natural light
Using different angles to get creative
Defining your sense of style
Learn about depth of field
Practice using helpful apps to achieve professional results

BECOME PART OF A CREATIVE COMMUNITY
Meet other like minded people to collaborate with on future projects
Learn to create your own online community
